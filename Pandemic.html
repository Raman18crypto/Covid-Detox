Can children or adolescents catch COVID-19

Research indicates that children and adolescents are likely to become infected as any other age group and can spread the disease. Children and adults should follow the same guidance on self-quarantine and self-isolation if there is arisk or have been exposed or are showing symptoms. It is particularly important that children avoid contact with older people and it's best to stay home and stay safe.

How long does the virus survive on surfaces

Coronavirus can easily be cleaned with common household disinfectants that will kill the virus. Studies have shown that the virus can survive for up to 72 hours on plastic and stainless steel, less than 4 hours on copper and less than 24 hours on cardboard. As, always clean your hands with an alcohol-based hand rub or wash them with soap and water and follow social distancing.
